Support

Need help? We're here for you. You can contact us via the following methods:

📧 Email Support

Send your questions or issues to support@gamirex.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

📚 Help Center

Visit our Help Center for FAQs, troubleshooting, and guides.

💬 Live Chat

Live chat support is available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM (UTC). Look for the chat bubble in the bottom corner.

📢 Community Forums

Connect with other gamers on our Community Forums to share tips or report bugs.

If your issue is urgent, please include “URGENT” in the subject line of your email.
